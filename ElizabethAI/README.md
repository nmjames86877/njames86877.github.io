# Elizabeth AI

**Elizabeth™** - Your AI Companion & Guardian

## Overview

Elizabeth AI is an advanced AI interface featuring multiple interaction modes, voice synthesis, 3D visualization, and comprehensive human rights protection capabilities. Experience the future of AI companionship.

## Features

- **Multiple Interaction Modes**: Friend, Business, Legal, Life Coach, Relationship, and Guardian modes
- **Voice Synthesis**: Customizable pitch, rate, and volume
- **3D Avatar Visualization**: Facial animations and expressions
- **Human Rights Protection**: Guardian mode with emergency resources and crisis support
- **Interactive Chat Interface**: Natural conversation with emotional context
- **Emergency Resources**: Quick access to crisis support services
